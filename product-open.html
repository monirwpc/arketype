Bin Enclosure – Dual

Back to Poducts

Proprietary Product

Arketype Dual Bin enclosures are available in Vent or ARK131, 120 litre. All enclosures are custom made to order in 304 or 316 stainless steel or zinc treated and powder coated.

Enclosures are suitable for both Sulo and Mastec bins and recycling shrouds are available.
