A is for Animals is an A to Z of animals in war. From mascots and messengers to creepy crawlies, family audiences will love exploring the remarkable stories of all creatures great and small.

A series of audio visual interactives allow the visitor to reveal “Roff”, the Doberman messenger dog, talk to “Cocky” from World War 1, match the noise with the animal and listen to a dog handler control an Explosive Detection Dog.
